## Recap Log

Full recap triggers after any 12 newly completed regular items since the latest full recap entry. It covers the older 6 items from that 12-item window, meaning items 12 through 7 back from the newest completed regular item, so the newest 6 items are left for a later cycle. If a full recap is unfinished and the user asks for a new item, ask for consent before continuing recap first. Daily recap is 3 random tests from previous items, with older items preferred.

## Exam Log

Monthly exam triggers after any 30 newly completed items since the latest monthly exam entry, counting both regular items and optional add-ons. A monthly exam is questions only: do not remind the user of item content before asking. Score the exam after completion. If the exam covers items that are also pending recap items, mark those recap items covered automatically.
